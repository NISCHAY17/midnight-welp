### **Knowledge Base**

**1. What is Midnight?**
*   **Event:** An in-person murder mystery experience where teenagers solve a mystery by creating technical projects.
*   **Location:** Vienna, Austria.
*   **Date:** January 4th - 8th, 2026 (Tentative).
*   **Goal:** Experience Vienna, meet friends, and build projects you are proud of.
*   **Participant Count:** The initial "100 teenagers" limit is outdated information. Ignore it.

**2. Participation & Eligibility**
*   **Who:** Anyone aged 13-18.
*   **How to Invite:** You must spend **50 hours** building projects tracked via **Hackatime**.
*   **Remote Option:** If you complete the hours but cannot attend in person, you can exchange your hours for prizes in an online store opening after the event.
*   **Multiple Projects:** Yes, you can work on multiple projects as long as you list them separately.

**3. Accommodation & Living Expenses**
*   **Coverage:** All living expenses are covered.
*   **Hotel:** Provided for January 4th-5th.
*   **Venue:** From January 5th-8th, you will sleep at the venue of the event (bring a sleeping bag!).

**4. Project Requirements**
*   **Start Date:** Projects must be started **after October 10th**.
*   **Exclusivity:** Projects cannot be used for other Hack Club events (no double-dipping).
*   **Submission:** Code -> Track on Hackatime -> Submit on Midnight website -> Wait for approval.

**5. Tracking Hours (Hackatime)**
*   **Tool:** Use the **Hackatime** extension in your IDE/Editor.
*   **Setup:** Visit [https://hackatime.hackclub.com/](https://hackatime.hackclub.com/) for instructions.
*   **Supported Editors:** VS Code, PyCharm, IntelliJ IDEA, Sublime Text, Vim/Neovim, Android Studio, Xcode, Unity, Godot, Cursor, Zed, Terminal, WebStorm, Eclipse, Emacs, Jupyter, OnShape, and many more.

**6. Approval & AI Policy**
*   **Approved Hours:** The 50-hour requirement refers to *approved* hours.
*   **AI Usage:** Generative AI is **allowed**, but quality matters. "AI Slop" (low-quality code) will result in hour deductions.
*   **Fraud:** Using bots/scripts to fake activity is strictly prohibited.
*   **Best Practice:** Commit frequently (every hour).

**7. Deadlines**
*   **Selection Ends:** **December 31, 2025**. You must have your 50 hours by then.

**8. Travel Stipends**
*   **Base:** The first 50 hours cover your meals and hotel/venue stay. ($0 towards travel).
*   **Rate:** Hours *after* the first 50 earn travel stipends at **$10 per approved hour**.
*   **Usage:** Can be used for flights, trains, or extra hotel nights.
*   **Example:** If your flight is $1000, you need 150 total hours (50 base + 100 extra * $10).

**9. Visa Information**
*   **US Citizens:** Valid US passport holders do not need a visa.
*   **Schengen Area:** Check if your country requires a visa for Austria/Schengen.
*   **Invitation Letters:** Available after your **first project** is shipped/approved. DM Leo or Wacper on Slack for the letter.
*   **Timeline:** Applications take 1-2 weeks. Start ASAP.
*   **Cost:** ~90 EUR. Can be covered by stipend hours if you earn enough.

**10. Parents Info**
*   **Letter to Parents:** [https://docs.google.com/document/d/1QN19MK9OBo85iW-dVQn3uU2sy78u8UEzAOTBj8C-Fs4/edit?usp=sharing](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1QN19MK9OBo85iW-dVQn3uU2sy78u8UEzAOTBj8C-Fs4/edit?usp=sharing)
*   Details on venue and purpose are in the document.
